Welcome to EchoSpective!

Thank you for considering participation in our survey focused on the exploration of generative AI image generation tools. This research is designed to gather insights into how individuals perceive, use, and contemplate the ethical implications of these advanced technological tools. Your participation will involve responding to a series of questions, which we anticipate will take no more than a few minutes of your time. Please be assured that all responses will be treated with the utmost confidentiality, and no personally identifiable information will be collected. Your involvement in this study is entirely voluntary, and you have the freedom to withdraw at any point without any consequences. Your valuable input will contribute significantly to our understanding of the societal impact of generative AI technologies.
